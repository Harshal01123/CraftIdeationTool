About the Artisan

Revanth Mora is a 4th-generation master artisan whose family has defined the clay traditions of Kondapalli for over a century. His work is not merely craft; it is a spiritual dialogue with the earth itself.

Specializing in high-fire terracotta and ancient firing techniques, Revanth bridges the gap between traditional iconography and contemporary minimalist form. Each piece undergoes a rigorous process of hand-kneading, sun-drying, and pit-firing that can take up to three weeks.

"The clay remembers the touch of the hand long after the artisan is gone. We are just temporary custodians of this earth."
